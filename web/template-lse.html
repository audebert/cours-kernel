EPITA Systems Laboratory

LSE is one of the three EPITA laboratories. Its goal is to provide students with an environment for learning and improving their skills in the following fields:

  • Design and implementation of operating systems
  • Development of device drivers
  • Development of system tools
  • Design and implementation of Domain Specific Languages
  • Technology watch in IT security

Surrounded by dedicated researchers, students take part in the following activities:

  • Organisation of courses and conferences on the EPITA campus
  • Development of open source software
  • Introduction of these productions during public events
  • Development for industrial partnerships
